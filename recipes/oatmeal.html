Opulent Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • Lidl Steel Cut Traditional Oats
  • 2% Milk
  • Peanut Butter
  • Oikos Triple Zero Vanilla Greek Yogurt
  • Fresh Strawberries
  • Fresh Blueberries
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Pour 1 cup of oatmeal into a bowl - 300 calories
  2. Pour 2/3 cup milk - 100 calories
  3. Microwave for 1 minute
  4. Add 2 tbsp of peanut butter and mix well - 180 calories
  5. Add 1 cup of greek yogurt and mix well - 90 calories
  6. Wash strawberries and blueberries
  7. Cut strawberries into thin slices
  8. Pour fruit into the oatmeal - 40 calories
  9. Drizzle the top layer with honey for a sweet kick - 100 calories
