Savory Salmon

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Salmon
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Lemon Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Paprika
  • Green Beans
  • Cilantro
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Let 1 pound of salmon sit outside fridge for 15 minutes
  2. Wash the salmon
  3. Cut salmon into 4 generally evenly-sized filets
  4. Place into a bowl and season front and back sides of the fish with
    • 1 tsp of salt
    • black pepper
    • garlic powder
    • lemon pepper
    • paprika
  5. Heat pan for 2 minutes and place 2 tbsp of olive oil
  6. Once the oil is heated up, place each filet down one by one
  7. Press down on each filet for 10 seconds
  8. Let the bottom, skin-side, cook on medium heat for 4-6 mins
  9. Before flipping, add green beans in the empty spaces of the pan
  10. Flip the filets with skin facing up and cook on low-medium heat for 5-7 mins
  11. Add cilantro to the top as it finishes cooking
  12. Voila, add 2 lemon slices and you are now ready to serve some beautiful, savory pan-seared salmon
