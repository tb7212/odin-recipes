Delectable Diced Chicken Breast

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Olive Oil
  • Cilantro
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Cumin Powder
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Clean two chicken breasts with cool water and cut off the excess fat
  2. Dice the chicken breasts into small squares
  3. Place into a bowl and season
    • 1 tsp of salt
    • black pepper
    • garlic powder
    • cumin
    • paprika
  4. Heat a pan for 2 minutes and put 2 tbsp of olive oil into the pan
  5. After oil is heated, cook the chicken on each side for 6-7 minutes on medium heat
  6. Flip the pieces with tongs
  7. When plating, add some cilantro for improved presentation and taste
